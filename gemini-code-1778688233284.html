About KJ Salons Kenya

Elevating Beauty & Wellness in Eldoret.

KJ Salons Kenya is a premium women-owned beauty salon in Eldoret dedicated to delivering luxury beauty and wellness experiences.

Located in Zion Mall, our salon combines advanced beauty techniques, expert trichology care, precision barbering, artistic nail design, and rejuvenating facial treatments to help every client look and feel their absolute best.
